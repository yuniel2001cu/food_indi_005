Chicken Tikka Masala

Contains baked chicken marinated with yogurt and spices - and masala sauce. Masala literally means "mixture of spices", so the dish has a very aromatic flavor. It is also cooked with tomato sauce and coconut milk.

Samosa

Fried or baked dumplings with salty filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, meat, or lentils. It can take on different shapes, including triangular, conical, or crescent shapes

Masala Dosa

It is a crepe like cookie rolled with potatoes, onion, curry, turmeric, coriander, rice and lentils. Filled with many more ingredients accompanied by a coconut chutney.

Bhelpuri

It is made of chickpea flour noodles, puffed rice and tamarind sauce.
